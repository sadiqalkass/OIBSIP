Biography

Birth

Alhaji Sir Tafawa Balewa was born in Decamber 1912 in Bauchi state in the Northern Nigeria protectorate.Balewa father,Yakubu Dan Zala,was a Gere and his mother fatima inna was half fulani.Balewa began his education at a koranic school in Bauchi,when southern colonial adminstration began to push for west education in the Northren region,Balewa was among the children sent to Tafawa Balewa Elementary School.Thereafter,he procced to Bauchi provincial school.Then letter studied at Balewa,then known as Katsina Collage.The Collage was several kilomiter from Bauchi,Balewa trekked for 10 days to go back home on holidays which is twice a year.Our beloved Balewa completed his five-year education in 1933 and returned to Bauchi to teach at Bauchi middle school.

Position Held

  • In 1941-1946 Tafawa balewa was the headmaster of bauchi middle school due to some unrest,investigation into student grievances indicated the former housemaster.
  • In 1947-1951 Tafawa balewa as lagislator,he was a vocal advocate of Northern Nigeria.
  • In 1951-1952 Togther with Sir Ahmadu Bello they proposed the transformation of the cultural organization Northern people's congress (NPC) during the election of 1951.Tafawa Balewa was elected as the Vice prsident of the new party.NPC won the plurality of the votes to the regional house of assembly in 1951.In the 1952 tafawa balewa enterd the goverment as the minster of works,and latter served as minster of transformation to coroprations.
  • In 1952-1965 Sir tafawa balewa became the frist prime minster.

Death

Our beloved nationalist was overthrown and murdered in a military coup of 15 january 1966.The circumstance of his death still remain unresolve.His body was discovered at a roadside in near lagos six days after he was outed from office.Balewa was discribe as modest and self-effacing.At his death,his major assets included his house in Bauchi and a 50-arce farm where he vacationed when he wanted to relax.The farm was located on the way to Tafawa Balewa's village about nine miles outside bauchi,many offical decisions while in office were taken at the farm.Balewa died and leve four wives and ninteen children.Balewa was buried in bauchi his nativeland at the age of 53 years old.
